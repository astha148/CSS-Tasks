FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The FIFA World Cup 2014 was the 20th edition of the prestigious international soccer tournament, held in Brazil from June 12 to July 13, 2014.

The tournament featured 32 national teams from around the world, competing in 12 different venues across Brazil.

The teams were divided into eight groups of four teams each for the group stage.
